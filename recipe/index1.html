Ingredients

  • 4 TBLSP of butter
  • Macaroni Pasta
  • 6 cups of water
  • Cheese
  • Sazon

Instructions

  1. Boil 6 cups of water in a pot
  2. Pour in Pasta
  3. Stir for 8 mins
  4. Pour water and mac into strainer
  5. Pour remaining pasta back into pot
  6. Add cheese and 4 TBLSP of butter
  7. Add Sazon
